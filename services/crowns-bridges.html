Bridges & Crowns

Single units to full-mouth reconstructions, crafted for fit, function and lifelike esthetics.

Our crown and bridge work combines digital precision with hand-finished artistry. Every restoration is designed in 3Shape, milled in-house, and finished by experienced ceramists.

What we offer

  • Full-contour zirconia crowns and bridges
  • E-MAX pressed and milled restorations
  • PFM and full metal cast options
  • PFZ with facial cut-back porcelain
  • Minimal-prep and no-prep veneers
  • 3D-printed or PMMA temporaries
