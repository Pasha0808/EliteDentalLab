FAQ

Frequently asked questions

Where is your work made?

Everything is produced in our own facility in Cockeysville, Maryland. Scanning, design, milling and porcelain layering all happen in-house, and every final product is made in the USA.

Which intraoral scanners do you accept files from?

We accept digital scans from 3Shape Trios, iTero, Carestream, 3M True Definition, Sirona Cerec Connect and PlanScan E4D. If you use a different system, contact us — we can usually find a way to connect.

What materials do you use?

We use only FDA-approved materials from trusted manufacturers, including zirconia, E-MAX, PFM alloys, titanium and premium acrylics.

Can you help me plan a complex case?

Absolutely. We offer case planning consultations and custom shade matching. Call us and one of our technicians will walk through the case with you.

How do I get started?

Just send us your first case — digitally from your scanner or by mail — along with a completed RX form. You can also call us at 410-667-7773 and we'll set everything up for you.
